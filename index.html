Welcome

Welcome, dear internet wanderer, to my small corner of the internet.

I've made this place to compile all my art, writing, stories, oc's, and interest in a nice, pretty place.

I like the idea of having my own website to post whatever I want without my posts being taken down by pinterest for the dumbest reasons, or to have a character limit in descriptions, or to have my art falsely tagged as ai by pinterest.

I also want something away from the noise of social media to call my own, to be unique to me. Plus, the idea of being your own web page with the ability to customise it in any way possible, with the only 'downsides' being I have to learn how to code it? There is no way I could pass that up.

Anyhow, I hope you enjoy this little website of mine, and maybe decide to hang around for a bit:).
